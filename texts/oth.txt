<p>The Oei Tiong Ham Hall was the old school hall. Its namesake, Mr Oei, was a Chinese Indonesian businessman who amassed his wealth from the sugar industry. Known as the “Sugar King of Java”, Mr Oei headed the Oei Tiong Ham Concern. It was the largest conglomerate in the Dutch East Indies during Indonesia’s early days of independence.</p>
<p>The Clock Tower, now a gazetted Singapore National Monument, was partially funded by Mr Oei.</p>
