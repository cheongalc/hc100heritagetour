<p>The Kong Chian Administration Centre is the hub that takes care of key administrative work of both the High School and College sections. It also houses the offices of the Principal, Deputy Principals, as well as other key personnel and is named after Mr Lee Kong Chian, who was Mr Tan Kah Kee's son-in-law. Mr Lee was also known as the “Rubber King”, and he was the Chairman of the Board of Directors of The Chinese High School from 1934 to 1957. He contributed substantially to Singapore’s cultural, education and other sectors by setting up the Lee Foundation which has provided financial support to the National University of Singapore, Anglo-Chinese School, Methodist Girls’ School and Tao Nan School, amongst others.</p>
