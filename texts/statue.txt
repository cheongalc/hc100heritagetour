<p>Of Peranakan descent, Mr Lim Nee Soon was one of the most prolific pineapple plantation owners in South-East Asia and was nicknamed “Pineapple King” for his success. He had attained great achievements with rubber planting as the government set aside almost 2000 acres of land for this purpose in Nee Soon, now known as Yishun, thus contributing immensely to our growing economy. Mr Lim was one of the school’s pioneers and served as the Treasurer and Chairman of the school. Similarly, Mr Lim’s son, Mr Lim Chong Pang, who also served as the school’s treasurer and contributed to the school’s development.</p>
