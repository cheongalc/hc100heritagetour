<p>Block B houses five lecture theatres and specialised tutorial classrooms for the Humanities Scholarship Programme, Chinese Language Elective Programme and the Bicultural Studies Programme. Quotes from prominent philosophers and Chinese leaders are also featured along the corridors.</p>
<p>The words ‘文心苑’ were written by Ms Sim Ann, Senior Minister of State, Ministry of Communications and Information and Ministry of Culture, Community and Youth, and a notable alumna of Hwa Chong's Chinese Language Elective Programme. In it, she expresses her desire for Hwa Chong students to have a better understanding of the Chinese culture so that they can become contributing citizens of society.</p>
